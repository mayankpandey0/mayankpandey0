### ⚡ Strategic Focus

**AI Developer & Computer Science Student** | **Agentic Workflows & Systems Architecture**

I build autonomous reasoning systems, high-precision retrieval engines, and developer tooling. My mission is elevating AI from conversational models to operational agents that can execute complex tasks with complete safety and program relevance.

* 🤖 **Agentic Systems:** Building autonomous task-loops using multi-agent frameworks (LangGraph) and state machines.
* 🧠 **RL & Latent Alignment:** Researching reasoning limits and Self-Distillation degradation in RL-based agents.
* 🔍 **High-Precision RAG:** Designing contextual indexing pipelines to enable semantic queries across codebases.
* 🛠️ **System Engineering:** Compiling type-safe schemas to establish zero-overhead Roblox execution environments.

> *"The future belongs to agents that act, not just models that predict. I engineer the logic that lets them execute safely."*
